About You-Yi

I am You-Yi Jau. (Yoyee) I am a graduate student in University of California San Diego (UCSD) since 2018 Fall, for M.S. in ECE department.

I graduated from EE department in National Taiwan University (NTU) in 2018. My research experience include odometry for AR system in Media IC & System Lab (Prof. Shao-Yi Chien) , pose tracking for AR in Media IC & System Lab (Po-Chen Wu) , and navigation for robots (Prof. Wei-Chao Chen).

I worked as research intern in Incentec AI center under Wei-Chao Chen in 2018, and as software engineer at Dell EMC in 2016. I aspire to apply my knowledge in the fields of education, health and environmental protection.
